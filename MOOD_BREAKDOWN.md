# SHADERSLOP DESIGNS — COMPLETE MOOD BREAKDOWN

**Catalog of all 175 files / 164 unique generative designs in this repo, analyzed at the source-code level and verified by live render.**

Method: every file was decoded (the sketch payloads are base64-embedded or fetched from `repo_script_js5_code`), its GLSL/JS math extracted, then rendered headless and eyeballed. What you get per design: what it actually is, the math inside it, the mood it puts out, a psychedelic-intensity tier, a trip-engine classification, website-fit roles, and keywords. 11 files are byte-identical duplicates and are noted with their canonical twin.

## LEGEND

**Psychedelic tiers** (score = math-signature analysis blended with live-render intensity):

- **BREAKTHROUGH** — ordinary reality is gone. Full-spectrum reality replacement. Landing-page material.
- **HEAVY** — a full trip. Dominates whatever page you put it on.
- **FULL TRIP** — unmistakably psychedelic, still composable with other elements.
- **COME-UP** — pattern salience and glow; strong texture, won't swallow your content.
- **MICRODOSE** — ambient shimmer. Background layer.
- **BROKEN / SKIP** — crashed, flat, or self-displaying source in headless render.

**Trip engines** (which perceptual logic the piece runs on):

- **LSD** — crystalline recursion, tracers, op-art interference, electric pattern-recognition.
- **Psilocybin** — organic intelligence: reaction-diffusion, Lenia, mycelium, breathing matter.
- **DMT** — hyperspace rupture: hidden dimensions, sigils, oracles, jeweled geometry, reality swaps.

**Roles** = suggested jobs on a trip-reports website (HERO, ORACLE/divider, AMBIENT, INTERACTIVE, GLITCH transition, FILLER).
